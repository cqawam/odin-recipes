Nigerian Moi Moi

Description

A steamed bean pudding made from blended peeled beans, peppers, onions, and oil, often enriched with fish, meat, or eggs. Itâ€™s soft, flavorful, and served as a side or main dish.

Ingredients

  • Beans (peeled)
  • Peppers (red bell & scotch bonnet)
  • Onions
  • Vegetable oil
  • Protein (eggs, fish, meat)
  • Stock cubes
  • Salt

Steps

Steps to Prepare Nigerian Moi Moi

  1. Blend: Blend beans, onions, and oil until smooth.
  2. Season: Add stock cubes and salt.
  3. Fill: Pour mix into ramekins or leaves, add fish/meat/egg (if using).
  4. Steam: Steam for 45-60 minutes.
  5. Serve: Enjoy warm or cold.

Done!
