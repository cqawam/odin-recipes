Nigerian Jollof Rice

Description

Nigerian Jollof is a popular West African rice dish made with tomatoes, peppers, onions, and spices, cooked in a flavorful tomato-based sauce. It is often prepared with long-grain parboiled rice and can include chicken, beef, or fish for added protein. Known for its vibrant red color and rich taste, it is a staple at celebrations and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Long-grain parboiled rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Peppers (red bell & scotch bonnet)
  • Onions
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken/beef/fish (optional)
  • Stock cubes
  • Garlic & ginger
  • Thyme, curry powder, bay leaves
  • Salt
  • Water/broth

Optional: carrots, peas, plantains.

Steps

Steps to Prepare Nigerian Jollof

  1. Blend: Blend tomatoes, peppers, onions, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Cook Protein: Fry or boil chikcen/beef (if using).
  3. Fry Base: Heat oil, fry tomato paste, then add blended mix. Cook unitl thick.
  4. Season: Add stock cubes, thyme, curry powder, bay leaves, and salt.
  5. Add Rice: Stir in rice, add water.broth, and cook on low heat.
  6. Simmer: Cover and let it steam unitl rice is tender.
  7. Serve: Garnish with fried plaintains or veggies (optional).

Done!
