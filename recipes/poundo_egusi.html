Pounded Yam and Egusi Soup

Description

A classic combination featuring smooth, stretchy pounded yam paired with a rich, nutty soup made from ground melon seeds (egusi), leafy greens, and fish or meat. A hearty and traditional meal.

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Egusi (melon seeds)
  • Spinach/bitter leaf
  • Palm oil
  • Stock cubes
  • Fish/meat
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Salt

Steps

Steps to Prepare Pounded Yam and Egusi Soup

  1. Cook Yam: Boil yam, then pound until smooth.
  2. Prepare Soup: Fry onions, peppers, and egusi in palm oil
  3. Add Protein: Add fish/meat, stock cubes, and water. Simmer.
  4. Add Greens: Stir in spinach/bitter leaf.
  5. Serve: Pair with pounded yam.

Done!
